Samosa

Description

A crispy, golden samosa is made by stuffing a spiced potato-pea filling (cooked with cumin, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala) inside a cone-shaped pastry made from a stiff dough of flour, oil, salt, and water. The filled cones are deep-fried until crunchy and served hot with mint or tamarind chutney.

Ingridents

  • Dough: 1 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tbsp oil, salt, water (as needed).
  • Filling: 2 boiled & mashed potatoes, ½ cup peas, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garam masala, ½ tsp red chili powder, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, chopped coriander.

Steps

  1. Dough: Mix flour, oil, salt, and water to make a stiff dough. Rest for 20 mins.
  2. Filling: Sauté cumin, ginger-garlic paste, spices, peas, and potatoes. Add coriander.
  3. Shape: Roll dough into circles, cut in half, form cones, and stuff with filling.
  4. Fry: Seal edges, deep-fry in hot oil until golden brown.
